About زعفران

Zaffran — meaning “saffron” — symbolizes luxury, aroma, and warmth. We combine the bold flavors of Indian cuisine with the charm of Arabic hospitality. From Hyderabadi biryanis to rich Mughlai curries, every dish tells a story of passion and heritage.
